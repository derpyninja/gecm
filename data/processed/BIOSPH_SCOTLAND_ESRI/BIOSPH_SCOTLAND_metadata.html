Abstract

Biosphere Reserves are areas of terrestrial and coastal ecosystems promoting the conservation of biodiversity with sustainable use. Biosphere reserves serve to demonstrate integrated management of land, water and biodiversity.

    Dataset background, process history

    Lineage, dataset history

    There has been only one batch of UK Biosphere Reserves which were nominated by the UK MAB Committee in 1976/7 and accepted by UNESCO. UNESCO approved the designation of Wester Ross Biosphere Reserve (BR) on 19/03/16. See UNESCO press release at http://www.unesco.org/new/en/media-services/single-view/news/20_sites_added_to_unescos_world_network_of_biosphere_reserve/#.VvPADk3FaM8 .
